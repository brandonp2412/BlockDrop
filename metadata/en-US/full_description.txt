Block Drop is a modern Tetris clone built with Flutter, bringing the classic puzzle game experience to your Android device.

Features:
• Classic Tetris gameplay with smooth controls
• Clean, modern interface designed with Flutter
• Responsive touch controls optimized for mobile
• Score tracking and line clearing mechanics
• Multiple tetromino pieces with rotation mechanics
• Progressive difficulty as you advance

Drop, rotate, and clear lines in this timeless puzzle game. Test your spatial reasoning and quick thinking as you arrange falling blocks to create complete horizontal lines.

This is a free and open-source implementation of the classic Tetris game, perfect for puzzle game enthusiasts who want a clean, ad-free gaming experience.
