Classic Block Puzzle - Free & Open Source
Stack falling blocks to clear lines in this timeless puzzle game! Arrange geometric shapes as they drop from the top of the screen to create complete horizontal rows, which will disappear and score you points.
Key Features:

Simple, addictive gameplay that's easy to learn but challenging to master
Smooth controls and responsive touch interface
Increasing speed as you progress keeps you on your toes
Classic arcade-style scoring system

Free and Open Source Software (FOSS)
This game is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden costs. The source code is publicly available, meaning the community can inspect, modify, and improve the game. Your privacy is protected - no data collection, no tracking.
